# Super Nintendo - Top 50

The SNES library is massive, but these 50 titles represent the absolute "cream of the crop" across every genre.

### The "Big Five" (Essential Masterpieces)

1. **The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past** (The definitive 2D Zelda)
2. **Super Metroid** (The birth of the "Metroidvania" genre)
3. **Super Mario World** (Arguably the best platformer ever made)
4. **Chrono Trigger** (Widely considered the best RPG of all time)
5. **Final Fantasy VI** (Epic story, incredible characters; released as *FFIII* in the US)

### Platformers & Action

6. **Donkey Kong Country 2: Diddy's Kong Quest** (The peak of the trilogy)
7. **Super Mario World 2: Yoshi's Island** (Beautiful "hand-drawn" art style)
8. **Mega Man X** (Fast-paced, satisfying action)
9. **Donkey Kong Country** (Groundbreaking 3D-rendered sprites)
10. **Super Castlevania IV** (The best whip physics in the series)
11. **Contra III: The Alien Wars** (Intense "run and gun")
12. **Kirby Super Star** (8 games in one; perfect for casual play)
13. **Mega Man X2** (More dashing, more bosses)
14. **Donkey Kong Country 3: Dixie Kong's Double Trouble**
15. **Super Mario All-Stars** (NES Mario games remade with SNES graphics)
16. **Earthworm Jim** (Wacky humor and great animation)
17. **Demon's Crest** (A dark, atmospheric hidden gem)

### Role-Playing Games (RPGs)

18. **EarthBound** (Quirky, modern-day setting; a cult classic)
19. **Super Mario RPG: Legend of the Seven Stars** (Mario meets Square Enix)
20. **Secret of Mana** (Action-RPG with a beautiful soundtrack)
21. **Lufia II: Rise of the Sinistrals** (Excellent puzzles and turn-based combat)
22. **Final Fantasy IV** (The first "epic" FF on SNES; released as *FFII* in the US)
23. **Terranigma** (Action-RPG; only released in Europe and Japan)
24. **Tactics Ogre: Let Us Cling Together** (Deep strategy)
25. **Breath of Fire II** (Classic Capcom RPG)
26. **Illusion of Gaia** (An emotional action-adventure)

### Fighting & Beat 'Em Ups

27. **Street Fighter II Turbo** (The arcade perfect experience)
28. **TMNT IV: Turtles in Time** (The best arcade-to-home beat 'em up)
29. **Killer Instinct** (Unique combo system and dark tone)
30. **Mortal Kombat II** (The best version of MK on the system)
31. **Final Fight 3** (The most polished of the Final Fight trilogy)

### Racing, Sports & Strategy

32. **Super Mario Kart** (The game that started the franchise)
33. **F-Zero** (High-speed futuristic racing)
34. **NBA Jam: Tournament Edition** ("He's on fire!")
35. **Super Punch-Out!!** (Satisfying rhythm-based boxing)
36. **SimCity** (Relaxing city builder with a great soundtrack)
37. **International Superstar Soccer Deluxe** (The best 16-bit soccer)
38. **Pilotwings** (A great showcase of "Mode 7" 3D effects)
39. **Uniracers** (Fast-paced unicycle racing; very unique)

### Arcade & Puzzle

40. **Tetris Attack** (A brilliant competitive puzzle game)
41. **Zombies Ate My Neighbors** (Fun, difficult overhead shooter)
42. **Star Fox** (Early 3D polygons using the Super FX chip)
43. **Sunset Riders** (Wild West arcade action)
44. **Wild Guns** (Steampunk "gallery shooter")
45. **Super Ghouls 'n Ghosts** (Infamously difficult, but rewarding)
46. **ActRaiser** (Half-platformer, half-god-sim)
47. **The Legend of the Mystical Ninja** (Co-op Japanese weirdness)
48. **Harvest Moon** (The original farming simulator)
49. **Pocky & Rocky** (Cute but challenging overhead shooter)
50. **Super Bomberman 2** (The peak of SNES multiplayer)
